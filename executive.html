Star Communications, Inc. consists of professionals with experience in the wireless, technology, logistics, transportation, retail, financial, healthcare and aerospace industries.

Kenny Golds

Founder, CEO

Kenny Golds has over 30 years of experience in the wireless industry across all verticals. Within his years of experience, the industry has changed drastically from the first handheld Motorola mobile phone to the plethora of smartphones today. Kenny has successfully adjusted Star’s business model to adapt to the fast-changing industry. Known as a "Highly trusted technology expert," Kenny has worked closely with top-level executives and Fortune 500 companies.

Kenny's areas of expertise include Mobile Devices, IoT, Internet Security, Technology Integration, Product Management, New Business Development, and Strategic Partnerships.

Some of Kenny's honors include:
• The Fulfillment Fund: Mercedes-Benz Mentor of the Year Award

• Palindrome Advisors-Founding Advisor: Palindrome links the nonprofit world with executive level leaders that give back through a number of programs, including but not limited to: serving on the board of a nonprofit (the Palindrome Pledge), mentoring the next generation of entrepreneurs, engaging in volunteering programs and nonprofit-based salons. Dedicating time and resources to building tools specifically for nonprofit partners.

• USA Hockey-Coach & Advocate

• Being a mentor, advisor, and coach has greatly impacted how Kenny leads the Star team. Kenny always acknowledges the hard work and dedication of his employees. He values encouragement and strives for a tight-knit team even through its recent exponential growth.

Kenny graduated from Michigan State University with a B.A. in Social Science.


Tony Martinez

Vice President, Partner

Tony Martinez has 25 years of experience in the wireless industry and has been a leader in driving the integration of wireless devices, wireless networks, and mobility software into enterprise, government, and small-medium sized businesses. Tony has been involved with the launches of RIM’s BlackBerry Solution on Bellsouth Wireless Data’s Mobitex Network, Good Technology’s launch of the Good wireless email platform on Mobitex as well as launches of RIM’s BlackBerry solution on converged voice and data networks such as T-Mobile, Sprint, Nextel, Cingular, Verizon Wireless & AT&T Wireless.

Tony has been instrumental in managing the roll out, deployment, and implementation of wireless products and solutions to several Global Fortune 1000, Federal Government, Financial, Healthcare, Manufacturing, Automotive and Entertainment companies. He has implemented customized applications and support services for several organizations.

Before joining Star Communications, Inc. Tony was Vice President of Sales at Karbon Systems. In 2002, Karbon Systems developed the first terrestrial wireless product to conform to the standards set by the DoD, offering full compliance with the Department of Defense (DoD) Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) and true end-to-end security using 256-bit Advanced Encryption Standard (AES). Prior to joining Karbon Systems, Tony held various positions with AirTouch, Bellsouth Wireless Data and Cingular. Tony has a B.A. from California State University Northridge in Cell and Molecular Biology and resides in Los Angeles, CA.


Matthew Schwartz

Matt Schwartz has over a decade of experience in the wireless industry with a strong focus on client services. Matt promotes positive outcomes and manages the relationship of our clients, in addition to managing the on-boarding phases of new accounts. Melissa González

Manager, TEM Services

Melissa González has been a part of the Star Communications, Inc. team for the past 4 years. Her expertise lies in mobility account analysis (including voice, messaging & data management), simplified reporting, cost allocation, expense & usage auditing and miscellaneous account management. She currently leads the Telecommunication Expense Management department for Star. She has efficiently streamlined reporting processes such as Cost Allocation for clients with complicated cost distribution needs. Her skills lie in optimizing accounts upon implementation & producing reporting techniques that save our clients time and money.

She also has experience procuring mobile device accessories & is a certified reseller. She has experience working with carriers such as AT&T, Verizon Wireless, Sprint & T-Mobile on a day-to-day basis & stays up-to-date on the most competitive carrier voice, data & messaging plans & features offered. She previously managed the international scheduling program at Star and has thorough knowledge on the best international options out there. Rachel Leeper

Marketing

Rachel Leeper has managed Star Communications, Inc.’s marketing efforts for the past 2 years. As Star has identified enterprises’ needs for advancement in wireless technology, she too has identified the need to spread the Star brand using modern marketing methods. With this drive, Rachel has built up from scratch Star’s presence in the digital world through social media engagement, blog posts, and email campaigns.
